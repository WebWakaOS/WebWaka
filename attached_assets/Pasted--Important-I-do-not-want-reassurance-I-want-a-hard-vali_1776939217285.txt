### Verification standard

Use a **bidirectional traceability approach**:

1. **Forward check:** for each important requirement, role, app, module, screen, workflow, and state listed in the report, verify that there is corresponding implementation evidence in code or docs.
2. **Backward check:** inspect the codebase and identify any implemented screens, routes, components, flows, roles, or state paths that were **not captured** in the report.
3. **Evidence standard:** do not treat a claim as fully verified unless you can point to at least one concrete source such as a route file, page component, schema, migration, test, or document.
