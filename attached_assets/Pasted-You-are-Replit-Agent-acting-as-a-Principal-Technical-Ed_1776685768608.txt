4. SPECIFIC RESOLUTIONS THAT MUST BE CANONICALIZED
==================================================

The merged v2 document must canonically reflect these decisions:

1. Queue consumer location:
- New dedicated appsnotificator Worker owns notification Queue consumption.

2. HITL escalation migration:
- Use kill-switch bridge pattern in Phase 6 via HITLLEGACYNOTIFICATIONSENABLED.
- Resolve fully to unified pipeline later, delete legacy direct dispatch in Phase 9.

3. WhatsApp template approval:
- Platform-operated WABA model for standard/default path.
- Platform submits templates on behalf of tenants.
- Enterprise partner-operated path may exist later without schema redesign.

4. Sender domain verification:
- Fall back to platform sender during pending verification.
- Track fallback in schema and delivery logs.
- Show verification status in brand-runtime.

5. Partner/Sub-Partner brand traversal:
- Standard walk to platform default.
- Sub-Partner may use brandIndependenceMode to skip parent partner.
- Flag is superadmin-controlled.

6. Retention and NDPR erasure:
- Delivery logs 90 days.
- Inbox 365 days.
- Audit logs 7 years.
- Audit rows retained but actorid and recipientid zeroed to ERASED.
- Other notification tables hard-delete relevant personal rows.

7. Digest timing model:
- Queue-continued global CRON sweep.

8. Partner admin notification surface:
- Shared inbox schema.
- category=partner surfaced in appspartner-admin.

9. USSD handling:
- SMS immediately outside session.
- In-app queued.
- Never interrupt USSD session flow.

10. Real-time inbox updates:
- Short-poll every 30s as default.
- SSE upgrade path documented, not default.

11. Low-data mode:
- Push suspended.
- In-app text-only.
- Email deferred.
- SMS for critical.

12. Sandbox mode:
- Strict redirect to test recipients based on NOTIFICATIONSANDBOXMODE and env-configured test addresses.

13. Webhook expansion:
- 30-event curated starter set.
- Full catalog for enterprise plan.
- 25 subscription cap on standard tier.
